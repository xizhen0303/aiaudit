Frequently Asked Questions

⚠️ Common Issues

Upload failing

Check your internet connection. Files must be under 500 MB each. Accepted formats: PNG, JPG, MP4. Rename the file using the correct format and retry.

App crashed mid-trip

Do not restart Uber. Check if the trip is still active in your ride history. Take manual screenshots of the relevant screens and note the crash time in your upload notes.

Wrong file uploaded

Contact the research team immediately with your reference number. Do not re-submit without confirmation from Mr. Barry XI.

Missed a screenshot step

Complete all remaining steps and note which step was missed in the upload notes field. Partial submissions are accepted — do not discard other screenshots.

📋 Reporting an Incident

If you experienced any unexpected events during your trip (confrontation, safety concern, app failure, or data loss), report it to the research team within 24 hours. Include your participant ID and reference number.
